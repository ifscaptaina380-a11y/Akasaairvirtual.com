Pilot Requirements

To join Akasa Air Virtual, you must meet the following requirements:

  • You must have an Infinite Flight Pro Subscription.
  • You must be at least Grade 3.
  • You must have an active IFC account that is in good standing.
  • You must be able to access Discord.
  • You must be at least 13 years old or above.
  • You must not be on the IFVARB Blacklist or Watchlist.
  • You must obtain at least 80% for the written test.
  • You must be able to log one flight a month.
