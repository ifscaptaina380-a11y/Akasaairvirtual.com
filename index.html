Welcome to the Virtual Skies of Akasa Air

Soar Higher, Together!

At Akasa Air Virtual, we're redefining the virtual skies with innovation, inclusivity, and excellence. As a proud member of the Infinite Flight community, we bring the joy of aviation to life through immersive experiences and realistic operations. Whether you're a seasoned virtual aviator or just beginning your journey, Akasa Air Virtual offers:

  • Authentic Routes: Explore a growing network of domestic and international destinations.
  • Modern Fleet: Fly the latest aircraft, including the state-of-the-art Boeing 737 MAX.

Ready to take off with us? Join Akasa Air Virtual today and experience the skies like never before!

Our Goals

At Akasa Air Virtual, our mission is to provide a realistic, engaging, and professional platform for aviation enthusiasts to explore their passion for flying. We aim to emulate the operations of Akasa Air Virtual with exceptional attention to detail, offering pilots an opportunity to experience the thrill and responsibilities of being part of a Virtual Airline.

Our goal is to foster a supportive and inclusive community that welcomes pilots of all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned veterans. Through innovation and teamwork, we strive to deliver a unique virtual aviation experience that blends realism, excitement, and professionalism. Whether you're flying a short domestic route or embarking on an international journey, Akasa Air Virtual is dedicated to helping you refine your skills, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and share the joy of aviation in a vibrant, collaborative environment.

Main HUBS

Kempegowda International Airport
(BLR)/(VOBL)

Located in Devanahalli, about 40 km from the city center, BLR offers excellent connectivity, modern facilities, and a smooth travel experience for both domestic and international flights. The airport features a state-of-the-art terminal with world-class amenities, including lounges, duty-free shopping, and a wide range of dining options.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport
(BOM)/(VABB)

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM) is one of India's busiest and most important airports, located in Mumbai, the financial capital of the country. Serving as a major gateway to India, BOM offers seamless connectivity to both domestic and international destinations.

Join Akasa Air Virtual

Welcome to Akasa Air Virtual! We're thrilled that you're interested in joining our growing family of aviation enthusiasts. At Akasa Air Virtual, we strive to provide a realistic, professional, and fun environment for pilots of all skill levels. Here's why you should join us:

  • Realistic Operations: Experience authentic flight schedules and routes tailored for realism.
  • Supportive Community: Connect with like-minded aviation enthusiasts through our active Discord server.
  • Career Growth: Earn promotions, achieve milestones, and build your virtual flying career.
  • Exclusive Events: Participate in exciting weekly group flights and events.

How to Join

  1. Requirements:
    • You must have an Infinite Flight Pro Subscription.
    • You must be at least Grade 3.
    • You must have an active IFC account that is in good standing.
    • You must be at least 13 years old or above.
    • You must not be on the IFVARB Blacklist or Watchlist.
    • You must obtain at least 80% for the written test.
    • You must be able to log one flight a month.
  2. Application Process:
    • Complete our Application Form.
    • Join our Discord server once your application is approved.
  3. After Approval:

    With approval, you'll receive access to our Crew Center, pilot handbook, and other resources to get started on your journey.
