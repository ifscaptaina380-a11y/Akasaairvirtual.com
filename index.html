About Akasa Air Virtual

Our Mission

At Akasa Air Virtual, our mission is to provide a realistic, engaging, and professional platform for aviation enthusiasts to explore their passion for flying. We aim to emulate the operations of Akasa Air with exceptional attention to detail, offering pilots an opportunity to experience the thrill and responsibilities of being part of a Virtual Airline.

Our goal is to foster a supportive and inclusive community that welcomes pilots of all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned veterans. Through innovation and teamwork, we strive to deliver a unique virtual aviation experience that blends realism, excitement, and professionalism.

Whether you're flying a short domestic route or embarking on an international journey, Akasa Air Virtual is dedicated to helping you refine your skills, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and share the joy of aviation in a vibrant, collaborative environment.

Begin your journey flying domestic routes, focusing on building foundational skills and gaining experience.

Entry Level

Gain experience on international routes

Mentor junior pilots and take on leadership

Starting from the first flight, pilots can climb the ranks by logging hours, completing flights, and showcasing their commitment to our VA. With each rank, you'll unlock access to new routes, aircraft, and opportunities that make the experience even more exciting.

Our Hubs

Kempegowda International Airport (BLR)/(VOBL)

Bengaluru serves as the main hub for Akasa Air Virtual, connecting pilots to numerous domestic and international routes.

Located in Devanahalli, about 40 km from the city center, BLR offers excellent connectivity, modern facilities, and a smooth travel experience for both domestic and international flights.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM)/(VABB)

Mumbai, our second hub, is a major city in our network, offering a wide range of domestic and international flights.

Known as the financial capital of India, it's a bustling metropolis that connects travelers to exciting destinations across the globe. The airport boasts two terminals with world-class facilities.

Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL)/(VIDP)

Delhi serves as a strategic hub, offering connections to both domestic and international destinations.

With its rich history and modern infrastructure, Delhi is the perfect gateway for pilots to embark on flights across India and beyond. Whether flying short domestic routes or long-haul international flights, Delhi is a vital part of our network.

Application Requirements

Requirements:

  • You must have an Infinite Flight Pro Subscription.
  • You must be at least Grade 3.
  • You must have an active IFC account that is in good standing.
  • You must be able to access Discord.
  • You must be at least 13 years old or above.
  • You must not be on the IFVARB Blacklist or Watchlist.
  • You must obtain at least 80% for the written test.
  • You must be able to log one flight a month.
