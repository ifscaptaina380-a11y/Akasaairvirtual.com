About Akasa Air Virtual

Our Story

Akasa Air Virtual was established in 2022 as a virtual airline community for flight simulation enthusiasts. We strive to recreate the experience of operating a modern airline with professionalism and attention to detail.

Our community brings together aviation enthusiasts from around the world to share their passion for flight simulation and virtual aviation.

Our Mission

To provide an immersive and realistic virtual airline experience for flight simulation enthusiasts, with professional operations, detailed procedures, and a friendly community atmosphere.

We aim to continuously expand our virtual network while maintaining the highest standards of virtual aviation.

What We Offer

  • Detailed virtual operations with real-world procedures
  • Regular events and group flights
  • Pilot ranking and awards system
  • Comprehensive documentation and training materials
  • Friendly and supportive community

Community Values

  • Respect: Treat all members with courtesy and respect
  • Professionalism: Maintain high standards in our virtual operations
  • Learning: Encourage skill development and knowledge sharing
  • Fun: Remember that flight simulation is ultimately about enjoyment
