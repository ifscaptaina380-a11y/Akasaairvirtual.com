About Akasa Air Virtual

Soar Higher, Together!

At Akasa Air Virtual, we're redefining the virtual skies with innovation, inclusivity, and excellence. As a proud member of the Infinite Flight community, we bring the joy of aviation to life through immersive experiences and realistic operations.

Whether you're a seasoned virtual aviator or just beginning your journey, Akasa Air Virtual offers:

  • Authentic Routes: Explore a growing network of domestic and international destinations.
  • Modern Fleet: Fly the latest aircraft, including the state-of-the-art Boeing 737 MAX.
  • Community Spirit: Join a vibrant community of passionate pilots and aviation enthusiasts.

Ready to take off with us? Join Akasa Air Virtual today and experience the skies like never before!

Our Goals

At Akasa Air Virtual, our mission is to provide a realistic, engaging, and professional platform for aviation enthusiasts to explore their passion for flying. We aim to emulate the operations of Akasa Air with exceptional attention to detail, offering pilots an opportunity to experience the thrill and responsibilities of being part of a Virtual Airline.

Our goal is to foster a supportive and inclusive community that welcomes pilots of all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned veterans. Through innovation and teamwork, we strive to deliver a unique virtual aviation experience that blends realism, excitement, and professionalism.

Our Rank Structure

At Akasa Air Virtual, we believe in rewarding dedication, skill, and experience. Our rank structure is designed to motivate pilots as they progress through their virtual flying journey.

Starting from the first flight, pilots can climb the ranks by logging hours, completing flights, and showcasing their commitment to our VA. With each rank, you'll unlock access to new routes, aircraft, and opportunities that make the experience even more exciting.

Trainee Pilot

Begin your journey flying domestic routes, focusing on building foundational skills.

Second Officer

Continue building experience with more complex routes and aircraft systems.

First Officer

Take on more responsibility with international routes and advanced procedures.

Captain

Reach the highest rank with access to all routes and leadership opportunities.

Rank Progression

Trainee Pilot to Second Officer 10 flight hours
Second Officer to First Officer 25 flight hours
First Officer to Captain 50 flight hours

Our Hubs

Kempegowda International Airport (BLR)

Kempegowda International Airport (BLR) is the main gateway to Bengaluru and a key hub in our virtual airline network. Located in Devanahalli, about 40 km from the city center, BLR offers excellent connectivity, modern facilities, and a smooth travel experience for both domestic and international flights.

The airport features a state-of-the-art terminal with world-class amenities, including lounges, duty-free shopping, and a wide range of dining options.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM)

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM) is one of India's busiest and most important airports, located in Mumbai, the financial capital of the country. Serving as a major gateway to India, BOM offers seamless connectivity to both domestic and international destinations.

The airport boasts two terminals—one for domestic and the other for international flights—equipped with world-class facilities.
